About Us

Who are we?

A group of motivated students who are willing to make a difference in the community through education.

Our purpose

The purpose of G.A.P.E is to provide students volunteer opportunities by leading with purpose and becoming educated about the multitude of obstacles children face in undeveloped countries. We encourage students to get involved and help towards realizing this cause.

Mission

We want to provide children the opportunity for proper resources towards their education and the representation regardless of their background.

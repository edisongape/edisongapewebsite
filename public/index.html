Who Are We?

Our Organization

To serve the community in a multitude of ways for the betterment of our local Community. Our values mirror that of our community, with emphasis on compassion, knowledge, and selflessness.

Our Mission

To establish an organization to give children in our locality access to proper education, resources, and representation regardless of their background.

Process

To give people volunteer opportunities by spreading awareness for primary education and allowing people to develop vital leadership skills. In addition, we want to give members of this club the ability to lead with purpose and learn about challenges kids in third world countries encounter. Through this club, we encourage people to help and promote this cause.
